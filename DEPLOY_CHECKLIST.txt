# Deployment Checklist (Xneelo)

To ensure your changes (especially the favicon) are live, follow these steps exactly:

1.  **Delete ON SERVER:**
    - log in to your Xneelo File Manager (or FTP).
    - Navigate to your public `public_html` (or wherever your site is hosted).
    - **DELETE** the old `index.html`.
    - **DELETE** the old `favicon.png`.

2.  **Upload FROM LOCAL:**
    - Open your local `dist` folder.
    - Upload **EVERYTHING** inside `dist` to the server.
    - Specifically check that:
        - `index.html` is the new one (check the date/time).
        - `favicon-v2.png` is uploaded.
        - `.htaccess` is uploaded (if your settings allow viewing hidden files).

3.  **Verify:**
    - Visit your website.
    - Press **Ctrl + F5** (Windows) or **Cmd + Shift + R** (Mac) to hard refresh.
    - If it's still old, try opening it in an **Incognito/Private** window.
